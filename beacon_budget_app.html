How it works

  1. Enter Projected Food Sales and Actual Sales by day.
  2. Enter weekly Budgets (Totals column only). Leave daily budget cells blank.
  3. Fill in daily Purchases per category (Meat, Seafood, Bread/Pasta, Produce, Pantry, Dairy).
  4. Over/Under shows: Budget Total - Purchases Total. If no purchases yet, it shows 0.

All numbers are treated as currency. Use positive numbers for purchases. The Totals column auto-sums days.
