How it works

  1. Enter Projected Food Sales and Actual Sales by day.
  2. Budgets can be entered manually (Totals column) or auto-calculated from sales using Budget Settings.
  3. Fill in daily Purchases per category.
  4. Over/Under per category shows: Budget Total - Purchases Total. If no purchases yet, it shows 0.

All numbers are treated as currency. The Totals column auto-sums days.
